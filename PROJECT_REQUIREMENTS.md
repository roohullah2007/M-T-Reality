# Real Estate Website Project — What I Want

Hi,

I want to build a real estate website. Below is everything I want on the website, written in simple words. Please read carefully before you give me a price.

---

## What The Website Is About

The website is for people who want to **sell their own home without an agent**. People can put their house on the website and other people can find it and buy it. We are starting in Oklahoma, USA.

---

## Who Will Use The Website

1. **Sellers** — people who want to sell their house.
2. **Buyers** — people who want to buy a house.
3. **Me and my team (Admin)** — we control everything from a special admin page.

---

## How The Website Works (Simple Flow)

1. A seller signs up on the website.
2. They fill a form with their house details and photos.
3. I check the listing and approve it.
4. The house shows up on the website for everyone to see.
5. Buyers search for houses and find this one.
6. Buyer fills a small form to contact the seller.
7. Seller gets the message in their dashboard and email.
8. They talk and finish the deal.

I also sell extra services like professional photos, videos, MLS listing, etc.

- I can also generate the letters as PDF and the QR codes.

- Sellers can download them.

### Media Orders

## Special Features I Want

- I can import many houses from Zillow.
- The website creates a special claim link for each.
- I print and mail letters with QR codes to the homeowners.
- They scan the QR or open the link, sign up, and the house becomes theirs.

### 4. QR Code Yard Signs
- Each house can have a QR code printed on a yard sign.
- When someone scans it, they go to the house page.
- Every scan is recorded so I know how many people scanned.

### 5. Open Houses
- Seller sets dates and times.
- They show on the house page.

### 6. Forms System
- I upload blank forms → seller downloads → signs outside (DocuSign) → I upload the signed copy back to their account.

### 7. Photo Upload
- Drag and drop many photos.
- Reorder them.
- Choose the main photo.
- Must work with iPhone photos (HEIC files).
- Photos load fast (use a CDN).

### 8. Search and Filters
- Search by city, address, or title.
- Filter by price, bedrooms, bathrooms, size, type.
- Sort by newest, price, size.

### 9. Address Autocomplete and Map
- When typing an address, suggestions show up.
- Map shows the house location with a pin.

### 10. Activity Log
- Every important action is recorded for safety.
